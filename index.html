We miss you scruff

Our Favourite Memories of Scruffy

  • One of Scruffy's favorite things to do was to go on car rides. Whenever we would grab our keys, she would get so excited and start jumping up and down. She loved to stick her head out of the window and feel the wind in her fur.
  • Scruffy was a great listener. Whenever we were feeling down, she would sit by our side and give us a sympathetic look. It was like she knew exactly what we were going through.
  • We'll never forget the time Scruffy snuck into the kitchen and stole an entire Toblerone bar off the counter. When we found her, she was lying on the floor with chocolate smeared all over her face. Despite the mess, we couldn't help but laugh at her guilty expression.
  • We would like to thank everyone who has supported us during this difficult time. Your kind words and gestures have meant the world to us. We would also like to thank Scruffy for the unconditional love and happiness that she brought into our lives.
    • Rest in peace, dear Scruffy. You will be forever missed.
