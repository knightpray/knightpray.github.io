About Mick Barwood

Mick Barwood, also known as "Big Willy", is a passionate sausage maker with over 20 years of experience. He is known for his high-quality 1kg sausages that are packed with flavor and made with only the finest ingredients.

Despite the size of his sausages, Mick is a humble man who takes pride in his work and is dedicated to making the best sausages possible.
